Help Center

Answers to the questions we get asked most. Can't find what you need? Reach out and we'll be happy to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Orders placed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day. Most deliveries arrive within 3–5 business days, and you'll receive real-time tracking as soon as your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied, return any item in its original condition within 30 days for a full refund.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as popular digital wallets. Every transaction is protected with bank-grade encryption.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, we'll email you a tracking link. You can follow your package in real time, every step of the way to your door.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Sustainability is at our core. We partner with eco-conscious suppliers and offset 100% of our shipping carbon footprint.

How do I contact customer support?

Our support team is available 7 days a week. Email us at support@novamart.example and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
